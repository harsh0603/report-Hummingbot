Hummingbot is open source software that helps you build market making and arbitrage bots that run on any crypto exchange,
centralized or decentralized.
Hummingbot is software that helps you build and run automated trading strategies, or bots.
Hummingbot bridges the centralized and decentralized worlds
With connectors to both centralized and decentralized exchanges, Hummingbot lets users arbitrage price differences between exchanges while improving overall market liquidity.
Humming bot is  a modular framework for building highly reliable  and high performance trading bot.
